Who We Are

In 2003, Women at the Well Ministries was birthed from a ten-year homeless outreach to
a 501c3 non-profit organization. Our Mission is to be actively involved in helping to
produce positive life-change and introduce hope to the underprivileged. Dedication
combined with commitment has been the passion of our mission, which is anchored
on a platform of perserverance and integrity.
