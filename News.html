School Vegetable Gardening Update!

       We here at WATWM would like to thank Florida Farm Bureau for their recent wonderful donation of seeds for our school kids to plant in their vegetable garden. As well as their donation of Tomato Plants, and Banana Peppers. Once the kids return from Spring Break they will be excited to learn about and plant Cucumbers, Radishes and Green Beans. Due to our local South Florida Weather, these are the best types of vegetable to grow this time of year here... In addition...we are growing purple eggplant seedlings to then transplant to the gardens as well!
